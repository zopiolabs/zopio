## How to Sign the CLA

### For Individual Contributors

1. When you submit your first pull request, a CLA bot will comment on your PR
2. Click the link provided to sign the Individual CLA electronically
3. Once signed, the bot will update your PR status
4. All future contributions will be covered by this single CLA signing

### For Corporate Contributors

1. Have an authorized representative of your company download and sign the Corporate CLA
2. Email the signed CLA to: [legal@zopio.com](mailto:legal@zopio.com)
3. Include a list of designated contributors (GitHub usernames and emails)
4. We'll confirm receipt and add your organization to our approved list
5. Your designated contributors can then submit pull requests without individual CLA signing
